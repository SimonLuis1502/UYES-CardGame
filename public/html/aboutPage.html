UYES!

by Andrii, Bilal, Daria, Simon, Srisharanya

About the Game

UYES! is a turn-based, web-based card game that we developed as part of our university coursework. Inspired by the classic UNO, players compete to tactically play cards and win the round – all directly in the browser.

The game supports real-time multiplayer, features a clean interface, and runs smoothly across devices. It’s designed to be quick, colorful, and fun – whether you’re playing on your own or with friends.

Our goal: combine simple rules with technical depth and create something that’s both enjoyable and technically solid.

About Us

We are five students from the DHBW Mannheim, currently studying Business Informatics (Wirtschaftsinformatik). As part of our studies, we were given the challenge to build an interactive browser game from scratch – both frontend and backend included.

We – Andrii, Bilal, Daria, Simon, and Srisharanya – decided on a UNO-inspired concept that combines rounds, multiplayer, and web technologies in a fun way.

Through this project, we deepened our knowledge in full-stack development, real-time communication (WebSockets), responsive design, and agile collaboration.
